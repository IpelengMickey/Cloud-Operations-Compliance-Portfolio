# Patch Management Procedure

## Overview

Patch management is a critical operational activity that helps maintain the security, stability, and compliance of cloud-hosted systems.

## Objective

To ensure systems remain protected against known vulnerabilities through the timely deployment of approved updates.

## Patch Management Lifecycle

### 1. Identification

Identify available patches and security updates.

### 2. Assessment

Evaluate the impact, severity, and urgency of available updates.

### 3. Testing

Validate updates in a controlled environment where possible.

### 4. Deployment

Schedule and deploy approved patches.

### 5. Verification

Confirm successful installation and system functionality.

### 6. Documentation

Record deployment activities and outcomes.

## Benefits

- Reduced security risk
- Improved compliance posture
- Increased operational stability

## Risks

- Security vulnerabilities
- System instability
- Compliance failures
